## 1.1 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (LATO SENSU)
This document delineates the technical forensic competencies available to the **Public Ministry of the State of São Paulo (MPSP)** for Civil Inquiries (Inquérito Civil) pertaining to critical infrastructure failures, aeronautical incidents, and complex software anomalies.

The objective is to translate computational evidence into legal proof ("Prova Pericial"), providing the Prosecutor with the necessary technical subsidy for legal action.

---

## 2. FORENSIC SERVICES PORTFOLIO

### 2.1 Aviation Incident Analysis ("Black Box" & Telemetry)
*   **Methodology:** Analysis of ADS-B/Radar logs for signs of Spoofing (NBR ISO/IEC 27037).
*   **Applicability:** Investigation of aerial accidents or near-miss incidents (TCAS Alerts).
*   **Deliverable:** Technical Report verifying data integrity and potential cyber-interference.
*   **Juridical Value:** Differentiation between systemic failure and malicious interference (Cyberattack).

### 2.2 Critical Infrastructure Software Audit
*   **Methodology:** Static and Dynamic Code Analysis (SAST/DAST) based on OWASP ASVS.
*   **Applicability:** Failures in public utility systems (e.g., Traffic Control, Hospital Management Systems).
*   **Deliverable:** Vulnerability Assessment Report (VAR) identifying negligence or strict liability defects.
*   **Juridical Value:** Attribution of technical responsibility to vendors/contractors.

### 2.3 Chain of Custody Verification (Cadeia de Custódia)
*   **Methodology:** Cryptographic Hashing (SHA-256) of digital evidence (Lei 13.964/2019 - Pacote Anticrime).
*   **Applicability:** Validation of logs provided by private entities to ensure non-repudiation.
*   **Deliverable:** Certificate of Digital Evidence Integrity.
*   **Juridical Value:** Assurance of evidence admissibility in court proceedings.
