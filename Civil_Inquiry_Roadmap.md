## 3. ACTIVATION PROTOCOL (SUMMONING)

### 3.1 Step 1: Appointment (Nomeação)
*   **Scenario:** A Prosecutor requires technical clarification on a complex system failure.
*   **Mechanism:** Automated query in the Expert Database for specific keywords (e.g., "Software Forensics", "Aviation Security").
*   **Notification:** Receipt of Official Summons (Ofício).

### 3.2 Step 2: Feasibility & Scope Analysis (Aceite)
*   **Procedure:** Technical review of the Inquiry scope within 48 hours.
*   **Deliverable:** "Termo de Aceite" (Acceptance Term) with estimated hours and resource requirements.
*   **Honoraria:** Calculated based on the official CREA-SP Table of Honoraria for Technical Services.

### 3.3 Step 3: Forensic Investigation Execution
*   **Toolchain:** Deployment of the "We Can Fly" Forensic Toolkit.
*   **Workflow:**
    1.  **Evidence Collection:** Application of Chain of Custody (SHA-256 Hashing) - *ABNT NBR ISO/IEC 27037*.
    2.  **Analysis:** execution of Anomaly Detection Models (Isolation Forest).
    3.  **Reporting:** Generation of the Technical Report (Laudo Pericial).

### 3.4 Step 4: Defense & Ratification
*   **Action:** Formal presentation of findings to the Public Prosecutor.
*   **Objective:** Clarification of technical causality for legal attribution.
*   **Outcome:** The Technical Report serves as the foundational evidence for the Civil Action (Ação Civil Pública).
